GestureDraw

Not the final name

I was watching a baby try to play with a fully featured drawing app, and they kept zooming in and out and panning way outside of the drawable area.  Kids, am I right?  Anyway, I made a toddler-proof app, and then got a little carried away adding more features into a debug menu anyway.  I'm currently working on moving those features over, so layers, color, and opacity can be manipulated without a menu.

The same baby then used this app to draw the picture here. If you squint, it kind of looks like a grim reaper crossing black velvet hills. Spooky!

But seriously, hopefully this will help some parents out there make a memorable keepsake once it's released to the app store.  For now, though, the code's available under a GPL 3.0 license.  It will be completely free, with no ads, because ads on kids' apps can be mildly infuriating.  At best.
